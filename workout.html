📘 Know Your Terms
🏋️
Compound Exercise
Works multiple muscle groups at once. More bang for your buck.
Squat, Bench Press, Deadlift
🎯
Isolation Exercise
Focuses on one specific muscle. Used for shaping and finishing.
Bicep Curl, Lateral Raise
🔁
Rep (Repetition)
One complete movement of an exercise from start to finish.
Lifting a dumbbell up and down = 1 rep
📦
Set
A group of consecutive reps performed before you rest.
10 reps × 3 sets = 30 total reps
📈
Progressive Overload
Gradually increasing weight or reps over time. This is how you grow.
Week 1: 10 reps → Week 3: add weight
😴
Rest Day
Your muscles grow during rest, not the workout. Required recovery.
Light walking or stretching only
